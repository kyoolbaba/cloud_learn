## After every mock — the 4-step review
1. **Read every wrong answer's explanation** (and "almost wrong" guesses).
2. **Log each miss** to `weak-topic-log.md` with the correct concept.
3. **Tag the root cause:** knowledge gap? misread question? careless?
4. **Re-study** the weak topic, then re-test those topics.

## Question-reading discipline
- Underline keywords: *most cost-effective, highly available, least operational overhead, real-time, FIFO, encrypted*.
- Eliminate clearly wrong options first.
- For "choose 2/3," treat each option as true/false.
